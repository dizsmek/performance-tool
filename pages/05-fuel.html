Read before use:

The software calculates the total amount as determined by EU OPS AMC2 CAT.OP.MPA.181 (Basic Fuel Scheme w/o variations). Final reserve and additional fuel are calculated according to 55% power setting fuel flow. The fuel flow unit is USGPH/engine. Headwind is (+), tailwind is (-).

In case of Power Setting 55% and altitude greater than 11000' check the chart in the POH.

In case of Power Setting 65% and altitude greater than 6000' check the chart in the POH.

In case of Power Setting 75% and altitude greater than 3000' check the chart in the POH.

For ATL calculation the DOM is 1300 kg (84kg pilot). Correct the result as necessary for the applicable DOM.
